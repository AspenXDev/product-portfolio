# Assumptions

In designing the proposed learning app improvements, the following assumptions were made:

## 1. User Behaviour

Users drop off primarily due to lack of early perceived progress rather than lack of content quality.

## 2. Product Context

The application already has sufficient content depth; the issue lies in engagement and retention mechanics.

## 3. Technical Constraints

The system is assumed to support basic UI changes such as onboarding flow adjustments and progress tracking indicators.

## 4. Business Constraints

There is willingness from stakeholders to prioritise retention metrics over feature expansion.

## 5. Data Availability

No detailed behavioural analytics were assumed available; design decisions are based on general product heuristics.
