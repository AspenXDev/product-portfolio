# Current State (AS-IS) – Manual Workflow

## Overview

The current workflow consists of multiple manual steps across different systems, requiring significant human coordination and repeated data handling.

---

## Flow

1. Data extracted from multiple sources manually
2. Data copied into spreadsheets for formatting
3. Manual validation and reconciliation performed
4. Output prepared and sent via email or internal tools
5. Downstream teams re-enter or reprocess the same data

---

## Key Pain Points

- High manual effort and repetitive work
- Multiple handoffs between teams
- Risk of data inconsistency and human error
- Lack of system integration
- Slow turnaround time due to sequential processing

---

## Observation

The workflow is not broken at the system level, but inefficient due to fragmentation and lack of automation layers.
