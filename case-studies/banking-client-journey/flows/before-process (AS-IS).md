# AS-IS Process – Current State

## Workflow

Client request  
→ Business intake  
→ Operations review  
→ IT validation  
→ Compliance checks  
→ Manual approvals  
→ Resolution

---

## Characteristics

- Sequential handoffs across teams
- Heavy reliance on manual coordination
- Limited transparency on status
- Multiple approval layers without parallel execution

---

## Pain Points

- Delays due to dependency chaining
- Repeated information handoffs
- Lack of end-to-end visibility
- High operational overhead for coordination
