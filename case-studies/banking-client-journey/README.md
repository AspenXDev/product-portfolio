# Banking Client Journey Improvement – Reducing Operational Friction

## Problem

Banking client journeys often involve multiple internal teams (business, operations, compliance, IT), resulting in fragmented execution, delays, and inconsistent service delivery.

---

## Context

This analysis is based on experience in banking transformation environments where client requests require coordination across multiple stakeholders and legacy systems.

The focus is on improving workflow efficiency within existing governance constraints rather than redesigning core banking systems.

---

## Key Insight

Inefficiencies are driven less by system limitations and more by:

- fragmented ownership across teams
- sequential processing of tasks that could be parallelised
- lack of end-to-end visibility
