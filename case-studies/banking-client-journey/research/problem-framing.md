# Problem Framing

## Problem Statement

Client service journeys in banking environments are often slow and fragmented due to multi-team dependencies and sequential execution patterns.

---

## What is observed

- Requests pass through multiple teams before completion
- Each team operates within its own system and process boundary
- Status visibility is limited across stakeholders
- Coordination relies heavily on manual follow-ups

---

## Impact

- Delays in service delivery
- Increased operational overhead
- Inconsistent client experience
- Difficulty in tracking end-to-end progress

---

## Goal of Analysis

To identify how the workflow can be simplified without violating banking governance or operational controls.
