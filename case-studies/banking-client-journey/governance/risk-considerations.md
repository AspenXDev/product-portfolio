# Risk Considerations

## Regulatory Risk

- All process changes must remain auditable
- No bypassing of required compliance steps

## Operational Risk

- Changes may create confusion if ownership is unclear
- Transition requires coordination across teams

## Adoption Risk

- Stakeholders may resist process changes
- Requires alignment across business, IT, and operations

---

## Mitigation Approach

- Incremental rollout
- Clear definition of ownership
- Maintain traceability of all process steps
